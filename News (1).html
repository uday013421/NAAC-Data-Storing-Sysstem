SIH 2023

Efforts are on at the country level for development in various sectors and entrepreneurship or to successfully face the problems of daily life. As a part of the same, Smart India Hackathon is organized annually by the Ministry of Education, Government of India and AICTE to explore new concepts and technologies that can solve these problemsFor the competition, the students in the field of technology are given various subjects and an attempt is made to find various solutions to the problems therein. The main purpose of this competition is to give scope to the imagination and knowledge of the students and to find new solutions to various problems in the country. This year smart vehicle, renewable energy, space technology, healthcare, communication, man-made waste disposal etc. Subjects were given. About 285 teams were selected for the first round of the tournament held at Lovely Professional University, Jaldhar, Punjab, out of which 5 teams were selected for the second round.

A team of students from the computer department of the college was selected in this. He presented the idea of recycling sanitary pad material in the competition. Then in the final round, he presented his innovative system by making actual project model under the guidance of experts and achieved success. The college team was declared runner-up and honored with a trophy and certificate by the university dignitaries The team included students Sujal Sawardekar, Subodh Muneshwar, Siddhesh Gurav, Suyash Pawar, Siddhi Salvi and Pranjal Sawant from the computer department of the college. College Prof. They were guided by Ashish Suware and Shreyas Dongre and congratulated the founder of the institute and former state minister Ma Ravindraji Mane, executive president So Neha Mane, principal Dr. Mahesh Bhagwat and the heads of all departments of the college, professors etc. for this success of the students.
